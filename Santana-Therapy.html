Therapist, advocate, badass...

I am a Latinx-queer identified therapist with experience counseling individuals from various racial and ethnic backgrounds and the LGBTQIA+ community. My work is trauma informed and social justice oriented.

I have my B.A. in Psychology from Florida Atlantic University, and my M.A. in Clinical Mental Health Counselling from Capella University. I completed my graduate internship at Mindful Moments Mental Health Counseling where I gained experience working with diverse populations.

My research and training background is grounded in Internal Family Systems as a treatment for working with survivors of trauma.

    • Session Fees & Pricing

    How much and how long are individual sessions?

    My fee is $150 for a 45 minute session.

    Do you accept insurance?

    While I currently do not accept insurance, I can provide documentation for out-of-network reimbursement.

    Do you offer sliding scale fees?

    I offer limited sliding scale availability based off of your income level. Please reach out to me if you are interested in sliding scale fees.

    How frequently will we meet for sessions?

    Sessions can range from twice weekly to bi-weekly. For the first few months of therapy it is important that we meet at least once a week to establish a therapeutic relationship. After that, sessions may switch to a bi-weekly schedule if preferred.
