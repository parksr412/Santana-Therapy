Therapist, advocate, badass...

I am a Latinx-queer identified therapist with experience counseling individuals from various racial and ethnic backgrounds and the LGBTQIA+ community. My work is trauma informed and social justice oriented.

I have my B.A. in Psychology from Florida Atlantic University, and my M.A. in Clinical Mental Health Counselling from Capella University. I completed my graduate internship at Mindful Moments Mental Health Counseling where I gained experience working with diverse populations.

My research and training background is grounded in Internal Family Systems as a treatment for working with survivors of trauma.
