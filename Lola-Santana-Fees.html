Session Fees & Pricing

I aim to make therapy as affordable and accessible as possible.

My fee is $150 for a 45 minute session.

I have limited sliding scale availability that starts at $80 and is based on your income level.

While I am currently not in network with any insurance plans, I can provide documentation for out-of-network reimbursement.
